## Emphasis

3 levels of emphasis are available to increase the contrast between important text and secondary or disabled informations.

### Black Emphasis

</div>

<div style={{ padding: "8px", fontWeight: "400", fontSize: "14px", letterSpacing: "0.15px", lineHeight: "20px", color: "rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.6)" }}>
  Black Medium Emphasis
</div>

<div style={{ padding: "8px", fontWeight: "400", fontSize: "14px", letterSpacing: "0.15px", lineHeight: "20px", color: "rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.38)" }}>

</div>

```scss

@import black-emphasis(medium);
@import black-emphasis(low);
```
